# Interview question packets

Mike-style sit-down prompts for the cities where the existing guide is
mostly research-built rather than lived-and-reviewed. Pattern matches
the Tirana interview that landed well in May 2026: open-ended, surface
the lived detail (arrivals, hotels, street rhythm, the specific bar,
the scams, the food), then weave the answers into the guide.

Read these before your next session in the city, or sit down for 20-30
minutes and answer them out loud. The exact wording of the answer is
the gold; the guide gets edited around it.

---

## Athens

**The arrival window.** What does ATH actually feel like? Big modern
airport, small one, jet bridges, low-cost shed? Did you take the metro
in or a taxi or beat the metered fare with a fixed rate? Did the X95
bus to Syntagma actually work, or is that one of those routes everyone
mentions and no one takes?

**The Plaka vs Koukaki call.** Where did you sleep, why, and what did
the neighborhood actually do at 9 p.m.? Is the Acropolis-view rooftop
hype real (Electra Metropolis, etc.) or is paying $50 over a normal
hotel for "the view" mostly the photo?

**Acropolis timing.** First slot, last slot, midday? Did the timed
entry work, or did the queue still snake? Any guide worth hiring or is
the audio app enough?

**The food register.** Souvlaki at a counter, taverna in Psiri,
Michelin-adjacent dinner in Kolonaki, all three? What did you eat that
you would send a friend back for, and what did you eat that ran tourist
prices for tourist food?

**The scam shape.** Cab from the airport, the "free Greek folklore
demonstration" near Monastiraki, the cocktail-bar tab switch on
Adrianou? Which of these did you actually run into?

**The day trips.** Did you do Cape Sounion, Delphi, Aegina, Hydra?
What was actually worth the train/ferry? Did the Hellenic Train
infrastructure ever do what the timetable said it would?

## Cairo

**The 3 a.m. arrival and the Giza vs Garden City question.** Where did
you sleep? The Marriott Mena House at the foot of the pyramids, the
Le Meridien Cairo Airport for a layover, or somewhere on the Nile? What
did the choice cost you in time and money over the trip?

**The pyramid day.** Drive there, walk there, camel? Did you hire a
guide, and was the guide worth it, or did you do the Wikipedia-on-your-
phone version? What's the actual time you should arrive and what's the
move when the trinket sellers swarm at the parking lot?

**GEM. Done, half-done, can't tell from outside.** What's the booking
experience like in 2026? Did you make it inside?

**The Common Giza scams.** Camel rides that don't end where they
started, the "free entry" at side gates, the guide who books your
pyramid ticket and pockets the difference. Which ones actually happened
to you, and which are theoretical?

**Khan el-Khalili.** Real shopping or just a photo op? Anywhere
specifically you'd send a friend?

**The food side.** Koshary at Abou Tarek, the Nile dinner cruises that
are mostly bad, the Sequoia/upscale-on-the-river circuit. What stuck?

**Getting out.** Aswan, Luxor, the desert? Any of those a real day
trip or do you have to commit to a multi-day add-on?

## Tokyo

**The flight in and the airport question.** Haneda vs Narita, and how
much it matters which one you land at depending on where you're
staying. The Limousine Bus vs the Narita Express vs the Skyliner.

**Where did you actually stay, multiple times if multiple visits?**
Shinjuku for the train hub and bright lights, Shibuya for the crossing
and Yoyogi, Ginza for the dressed-up version, Asakusa for the temple
side? Specific hotels you'd repeat or specifically wouldn't.

**The convenience store conversation.** Lawson, FamilyMart, 7-Eleven.
Which one and why, and what's the breakfast move from a konbini?

**Trains.** Suica vs IC card vs the Pasmo. How much of central Tokyo
is actually JR Yamanote walkable, and where do you need to switch to
the Metro?

**The food specifics.** Where do you actually eat ramen, where do you
queue 90 minutes vs walk past, what's the omakase question in Tsukiji
vs Ginza, and is the depachika (basement food hall) circuit the
underrated cheap dinner most travelers miss?

**The day-trip question.** Hakone, Nikko, Kamakura, Yokohama, Mt Fuji.
Which one earns the JR Pass day?

**The scam shape (it's a soft one in Tokyo).** Roppongi club touts,
the Kabukicho bar-bill swap, the temple-front "donation" pitch. What
did you actually run into?

## Seoul

**Arrival from ICN.** AREX express vs commuter vs limousine bus? Which
hotel zones does each one actually serve well? Did you take a taxi at
2 a.m. on arrival and was that the right call?

**Where did you sleep?** Myeongdong for the shopping spine, Itaewon
for the bar-and-international scene, Gangnam for the new build, Bukchon
Hanok village for the slow-side stay? What did each base actually feel
like at night?

**Korean BBQ vs the rest.** Which BBQ spot do you actually go back to,
and what's the difference between the tourist version near Myeongdong
and the worker version in the back lanes? Did you do anjip-style
drinking food alongside soju nights, and what worked?

**The skincare and shopping circuit.** Olive Young, the Hongdae
boutiques, the Apgujeong department-store wall. Anything that
specifically beats just hitting an Olive Young?

**Seoul Subway.** T-Money card, the ride-quality compared to Tokyo,
the late-night cutoff. What's the rhythm?

**Day trips.** DMZ tour. Worth it, run by whom, what's the actual JSA
question in 2026 given the back-and-forth on the border?

**Cafe culture.** Seoul has the deepest cafe culture of any Asian
capital. Where do you go, what kind of cafes, do you have a favorite
specialty roaster?

## Bali (Ubud + Canggu split)

**The arrival into DPS and the immediate question.** Was the visa-on-
arrival queue 90 minutes? Did you use Bluebird from the airport or pre-
book? How does a Canggu vs Ubud first-night decision actually shake
out?

**Where do you sleep on a 7- to 10-day Bali trip?** The Ubud-then-
coast split, the Ubud-only "slow Bali" version, the surfer-or-DJ
Canggu-only version, or the upscale Seminyak/Uluwatu version? Mike's
actual rotation.

**The scooter question.** Do you rent one, and at what comfort level?
Where did you actually crash or come close? How do the Indonesian
licensing and insurance shapes work for tourists?

**The food split.** Warungs (the cheap local), the Eat Pray Love
Ubud-cafe scene (overpriced photos), and the actual standout
restaurants. Locavore, Mosaic, etc. Which ones earn the tasting menu?

**Temple etiquette and the scams.** Sarong rentals at the temple
gates, the "you must hire a guide" framing at Tirta Empul, the
monkey-grabs-your-sunglasses pattern at Uluwatu. Which ones bit?

**The waterfall day.** Which one, by what route, and is the photo
worth the hike or did you get there at 11 a.m. with 200 other Aussies?

**Day trips.** Nusa Penida (the Kelingking photo), Lombok, Gili
Trawangan. Real or skip on a first Bali trip?

## Chiang Mai

**The arrival window and the city shape.** Did CNX feel small after a
big-city flight in? Songthaew negotiations from the airport, Grab,
hotel transfer? Which gate of the old town did your hotel end up
closest to, and did that matter?

**The two-rhythm question.** Old town inside the moat vs Nimman just
outside. How does the trip change between the two? Where did you
actually stay, and which one would you tell a friend?

**The temples and the Doi Suthep day.** Wat Phra That Doi Suthep
sunrise vs midday, the Wat Pha Lat secret-temple walk, the elephant-
sanctuary question (which sanctuary is actually ethical, and how do
you tell)?

**The food.** Khao soi spots, the night-market food (Sunday Walking
Street vs Saturday vs the Sunday Walking Street again), the cheap
massage-and-Pad-Thai loops in the moat. What's the actual order at
Khao Soi Khun Yai vs Khao Soi Mae Sai?

**Songkran in Chiang Mai.** If you've been there for it, this is the
single biggest planning point in the guide. What does it actually feel
like, and is it worth either pushing the trip a week or planning
specifically around it?

**Pai and the mountain side.** The 762-curve van ride. Worth doing
once, or skip and stay in Chiang Mai?

## Bath

**Arrival from London.** GWR from Paddington vs the Bristol-and-back
shuffle. Which station, which line, what does the platform-to-hotel
walk actually look like?

**Where to sleep.** The Royal Crescent area vs the city center vs
near the station? The Gainsborough Bath Spa pin question.

**The Roman Baths.** Booking pattern in 2026, audio guide vs Wikipedia
walk, the evening Torchlit Roman Baths visit (worth it or marketing)?

**Thermae Bath Spa next door.** This is the underused angle. Did you
actually use it as a contrast to the Roman museum, and how does the
rooftop pool look from the inside?

**Food and pub picks.** Sally Lunn's (the famous bun), the Sunday
roast at one specific pub, the gastropub scene. The non-touristy place
within a 10-minute walk of the Pump Room.

**Day trips out.** Stonehenge from Bath (covered in
`/lists/salisbury-stonehenge`), Wells, Castle Combe, the Cotswolds.
Which one was actually worth the half-day from a Bath base?

## York

**The train in from London and Edinburgh.** LNER from Kings Cross, the
position of the station relative to the old walls, the walk to the
center.

**The walls walk.** Full loop or partial? What time of day, and which
gate (bar) is the start point you came to prefer?

**The Shambles and the tourist crush.** What time does it actually
empty out? Did the Harry Potter foot traffic ruin it, or is there a
window before 10 a.m. that still works?

**Jorvik Viking Centre, York Minster, the National Railway Museum,
Clifford's Tower.** Which ones earn the entry fee, in what order, and
where do you book ahead?

**Food.** York's food scene is underrated. Specific pubs, specific
restaurants, the Sunday roast question, the chocolate-history side
(Bettys?).

**Day trips.** Whitby, Castle Howard, the Yorkshire Dales, the North
York Moors railway. Which is actually a day trip and which is its own
weekend?

## Manchester

**Arrival from London or MAN airport.** Avanti West Coast vs LNER vs
the new Liz line connection. Where does the train let you out and how
does the airport tram work?

**The base question.** Northern Quarter vs Spinningfields vs Castlefield
vs Salford Quays? What's the food-and-bars density at each?

**The football question.** Old Trafford and the Etihad. Does the
stadium tour earn the time when there isn't a match? What's the
match-day base, and how do prices spike?

**Music history walks.** The Hacienda site, the Joy Division /
Smiths walking circuit, Salford Lads Club. Which of these are real and
which are tourist plaques?

**Northern Quarter food.** This is where Manchester rebuilt itself.
Which restaurants, which bakeries, which coffee shops are doing the
work?

**The Curry Mile.** Worth the trek to Rusholme, or just stay in the
Northern Quarter?

## Workflow for the interview

1. Read one packet before bed the night before the session.
2. Sit with a recorder (phone Voice Memos is fine) for 20-30 minutes
   and answer the questions out loud. Don't write, just talk.
3. Paste the transcript into a new commit on the guide as plain prose
   in the authoring-notes block.
4. I (the assistant) take the transcript on the next pass and shape
   it into the guide sections, preserving Mike's actual phrasing
   wherever it lands.

The Tirana run was the proof. Same shape works for the rest.
