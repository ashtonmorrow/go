# Considering Dubrovnik but scared off by crowds? Why not stay in Herceg Novi instead?

A visit to King's Landing is a bucket list item for many. What's not to enjoy? You've seen the TV show so I don't need to sell you on the scenery. However, once there many are turned off by massive crowds which multiply when cruise ships dock. 

The city has had such an issue with overtourism that similar to Venice, they've started adding in various mechanisms to discourage tourists. For instance, you are no longer allowed to carry your luggage in if you are visiting the old town. Instead you must hire a porter to bring your bags in. 

For the misanthropes and crowd averse I'll let you in on a little secret that will no doubt stay a secret for long. You can simply stay a little bit further south in Montenegro and enjoy all of the same highlights that brought you to Croatia as a holiday destination in the first place. 

In fact, you can even fly in and out of Dubrovnik airport, it's only 45 minutes away. The best part, you'll both save some money and be able to see Dubrovnik at your own pace.
