[Bar](/cities/bar-mne) is the south Adriatic port town of Montenegro, the practical and cheaper alternative to staying in Kotor or Budva. Ferries to Italy leave from here. This list is short and mostly food.

## Where to stay

[Hotel Pharos](/pins/hotel-pharos) is the hotel on this list.

## Where to eat

The eating rotation runs casual and Balkan. [Ćevabdžinica Goranac](/pins/cevabdzinica-goranac) is the cevapi spot, [Gurman](/pins/gurman) and [Ribar 2](/pins/ribar-2) are the Montenegrin sit-down picks, and [fish grill montenegro](/pins/fish-grill-montenegro) is the seafood option. For a casual mix, [i Pizza](/pins/i-pizza) handles pizza, [King Gyros](/pins/king-gyros) handles the Greek hand-held, and [Dublin Pub](/pins/dublin-pub) is the beer stop. [Riva Beach Bar](/pins/riva-beach-bar-recently-viewed) and [YOUR TIME](/pins/your-time) round out the list.
