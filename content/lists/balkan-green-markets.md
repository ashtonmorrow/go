This is a working list of green markets I would use as orientation points on longer stays in the Balkans. Some are still mostly everyday food markets. Others sit close enough to old towns or visitor routes that they are partly part of the sightseeing circuit now.

I would use the list practically: stop by early, buy fruit, honey, greens, or something small to bring back to an apartment, then decide whether the market still feels useful for ordinary shopping or mainly worth a short look.
