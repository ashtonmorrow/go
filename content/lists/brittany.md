Brittany is the peninsula at the northwest corner of France, with a coastline of walled ports, tidal islands, and Channel weather. This list sits in [Saint-Malo](/cities/saint-malo), the granite corsair town behind the ramparts.

## What's on this list

[Grand Bé](/pins/grand-be) is the tidal island off the ramparts, reachable on foot at low tide and cut off when the water comes in, so check the tide table before you walk out. For food, [L'Amiral](/pins/l-amiral-saint-malo) and [La Trinquette](/pins/la-trinquette) are the two old-town restaurants on this list.
