[Hoi An](/cities/hoi-an) is the old trading port on the central Vietnam coast, a UNESCO-listed town of low timber shophouses and lantern-lit streets. Two things draw most visitors: the old town itself and the tailors who make clothes to measure in a day or two. This list covers both.

## What's on this list

[Ba Ri Tailor](/pins/ba-ri-tailor) is the made-to-measure tailor on this list. Allow time for a fitting and a second visit before you leave town. [Jack's Cat Cafe](/pins/jack-s-cat-cafe) is a cat cafe where entry includes a drink.
