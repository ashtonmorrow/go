[Cadaqués](/cities/cadaques) is the whitewashed village on the far side of the Cap de Creus headland, the easternmost point of mainland Spain. The drive in is a slow mountain road, which keeps the day-tripper crowds down. Salvador Dalí's house is in the next cove at Portlligat. This list covers where to eat.

## Where to eat

[Cadaq's](/pins/cadaq-s) and [EL VERDUGO](/pins/el-verdugo) are the two restaurants on this list.
