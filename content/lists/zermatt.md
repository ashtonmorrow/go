[Zermatt](/cities/zermatt) is the car-free village at the foot of the Matterhorn, reached by the cogwheel train from Täsch. Most of a Zermatt trip is the mountain itself: the Gornergrat railway, the cable-car network, and the trails. This list covers where to eat, in the village and on the slope.

## Where to eat

[Restaurant Zum See](/pins/restaurant-zum-see) and [Spycher](/pins/spycher) sit on the mountain side of a day out, the kind of stop you build a hike or a ski run around, so book ahead. [Restaurant Arvenstube](/pins/restaurant-arvenstube) is the village option for a sit-down dinner after coming down.
