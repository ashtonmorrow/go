[Taipei](/cities/taipei) is one of the great noodle cities and one of the cheaper food cities in Asia. This list is short: two beef-noodle and homemade-noodle stops that lived up to "close and cheap" on a wandering day.

## Where to eat

[Niou Dien Beef Noodles](/pins/niou-dien-beef-noodles) is the beef-noodle bowl. [Lao Shandong Homemade Noodles](/pins/lao-shandong-homemade-noodles) is the hand-pulled-noodles spot with a Shandong-province lean. Both are casual sit-down rooms at the price of a Starbucks coffee back home.
