[Saint-Malo](/cities/saint-malo) and Mont Saint-Michel sit about an hour apart on the border of Brittany and Normandy, and most trips pair them. Saint-Malo is the walled granite corsair port; Mont Saint-Michel is the abbey on a tidal island. This list has a stop for each.

## What's on this list

[Au Coup De Canon](/pins/au-coup-de-canon) is a restaurant inside the Saint-Malo ramparts. [Hôtel Vert](/pins/hotel-vert-hotel-au-mont-saint-michel) is a hotel at the Mont Saint-Michel access village, which puts you close enough to walk to the abbey before the day-trip buses arrive.
