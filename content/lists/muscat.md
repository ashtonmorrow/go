[Muscat](/cities/muscat) is the capital of Oman, a low-rise city strung along the coast between the Gulf of Oman and the bare Hajar mountains. The old port district of Mutrah, with its corniche and souq, is the part most visitors walk. This list has two stops there.

## What's on this list

[Bait Al Luban](/pins/bait-al-luban-omani-restaurant-mutrah) is an Omani restaurant on the Mutrah corniche, the place to try the national dishes. [The W6 Balcony Walk](/pins/w6-balcony-walk-hike) is the marked trail that climbs above the corniche. Start early and carry water.
