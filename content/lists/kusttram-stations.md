Belgium's Kusttram is not something I would build a whole trip around unless you are specifically into trams. I like it more as a practical coastal trick: if you are already near Oostende, Bruges, Ghent, or the Flemish coast, you can ride a section, get off somewhere, walk by the water, and let the day stay loose.

This is my atlas list of Kusttram station pins, not the official timetable. De Lijn is still the place to check current service, works, and stop changes before you travel.
