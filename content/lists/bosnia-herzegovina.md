Bosnia and Herzegovina sits in the western Balkans, the country of the Ottoman and Habsburg layered cities, the karst rivers, and the bridges. This list is the country-level rollup. [Mostar](/cities/mostar) is the better-known stop and has its own guide at [/lists/mostar](/lists/mostar). The pin set here covers the Old Bridge and one Jajce stop.

## What's on this list

[Old Bridge](/pins/old-bridge) is the rebuilt 16th-century Ottoman bridge over the Neretva in Mostar, the symbol of the city and a UNESCO site. [Kriva ćuprija](/pins/kriva-cuprija) is the smaller older bridge upstream, a half-block walk and a quieter photograph. [Fortica](/pins/fortica) is the medieval fortress above the city for the view.

Outside Mostar, [Pliva Springs](/pins/pliva-springs) is the karst spring complex near Jajce in the central highlands, the kind of clear-water natural area the country specializes in.
