[Cambridge](/cities/cambridge) is the English university city about an hour north of London by train, built around the colleges, the Backs, and the River Cam. This list covers where to eat.

## Where to eat

[Arepa's Station](/pins/arepa-s-station) is a Venezuelan spot, and [The Clarendon Arms](/pins/the-clarendon-arms) is a traditional pub. Both are on this list.
