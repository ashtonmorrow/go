Jamaica covers two different trips in one country: the cool, green Blue Mountains above Kingston and the flat west-coast beach towns. This list has one place to stay in each.

## Where to stay

[RafJam Bed and Breakfast](/pins/rafjam-bed-and-breakfast) is in Irish Town, up in the Blue Mountains coffee country above Kingston. [Seastar Inn](/pins/seastar-inn) is in Negril, on the long west-coast beach at the other end of the island.
