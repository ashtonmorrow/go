Como sits at the southwest tip of Lake Como, where the Alps drop straight into the water. It is the practical base for the lake, a 40-minute train from Milan, with the ferry network fanning out from the waterfront. This list covers where to eat in the town.

## Where to eat

[KOMO Fusionable Taste](/pins/komo-fusionable-taste), [La Vita è Bella](/pins/la-vita-e-bella-ristorante-insalateria), and [Ristorante Sottovoce](/pins/ristorante-sottovoce) are the three Como restaurants on this list. Lakeside tables fill in the evening, so reserve.
