[Santiago de Compostela](/cities/santiago-de-compostela) is the Galician city where the Camino de Santiago ends, built around the cathedral that holds the shrine of Saint James. The granite old town is compact and walkable. This list collects three stops outside the cathedral itself.

## What's on this list

[Mercado de Abastos](/pins/mercado-de-abastos-de-santiago-de-compostela) is the city's produce market, the second most visited site in town after the cathedral. [Parque de San Domingos de Bonaval](/pins/parque-de-san-domingos-de-bonaval) is a terraced park on the old convent grounds with a view back over the rooftops. [Praza da Quintana](/pins/praza-da-quintana-de-vivos) is the square directly behind the cathedral, used for the evening passeggiata.
