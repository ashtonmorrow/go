[Alicante](/cities/alicante)'s tram is the trick that makes the city feel larger than it is. The center is compact, but the network lets you stay near the old town or Postiguet and still spend a beach day farther up the Costa Blanca without renting a car.

I would use it less as a commuter map and more as a low-pressure coastal tool. El Campello is the easy first ride. If you want smaller coves, keep looking north toward Coveta Fuma and Cala Piteres. Benidorm is the long obvious target, partly because you can transfer there if you want to continue farther up the line.

This is my station-pin index, not an official timetable. Check TRAM d'Alacant before traveling for current times, transfer rules, service works, and ticket details.
